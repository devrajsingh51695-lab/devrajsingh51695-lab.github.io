🍓 Strawberry Protein Smoothie

A sweet and refreshing high-protein smoothie perfect for breakfast, muscle recovery, and healthy snacking.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • 4-5 ice cubes

Nutrition (Approx.)

  • Protein: 31g
  • Calories: 330
  • Carbohydrates: 27g
  • Fat: 7g

Instructions

  1. Add strawberries, milk, yogurt and protein powder to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again.
  4. Serve chilled.

Benefits

  • Rich in Vitamin C
  • Supports muscle recovery
  • High in protein
  • Perfect post-workout smoothie
