🥭 Mango Protein Smoothie

Refreshing, high-protein smoothie perfect for breakfast and post-workout recovery.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ripe mango chunks
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • 4-5 ice cubes

Nutrition (Approx.)

  • Protein: 32g
  • Calories: 350
  • Carbohydrates: 35g
  • Fat: 7g

Instructions

  1. Add mango, milk, yogurt and protein powder to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again.
  4. Serve chilled.

Benefits

  • Rich in Vitamin C
  • Supports muscle recovery
  • Naturally sweet and refreshing
  • High-protein healthy snack
