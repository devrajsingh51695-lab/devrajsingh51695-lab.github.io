🥭 Mango Protein Smoothie

Refreshing, tropical, and packed with protein. Perfect for breakfast, muscle recovery, and a healthy post-workout drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ripe mango chunks
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • 4–5 ice cubes

Nutrition (Approx.)

  • Protein: 32g
  • Calories: 350
  • Carbohydrates: 35g
  • Fat: 7g

Instructions

  1. Add mango, milk, yogurt and protein powder to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again.
  4. Serve chilled.

Benefits

  • Rich in Vitamin C.
  • Supports muscle recovery.
  • Naturally sweet and refreshing.
  • High-protein healthy snack.
