🍫🥜 Chocolate Peanut Butter Protein Smoothie

A rich and creamy protein smoothie loaded with chocolate flavor, peanut butter, and muscle-building protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4-5 ice cubes

Nutrition (Approx.)

  • Protein: 40g
  • Calories: 480
  • Carbohydrates: 36g
  • Fat: 18g

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again.
  4. Serve chilled.

Benefits

  • 40g high-quality protein
  • Great for muscle gain and recovery
  • Provides healthy fats from peanut butter
  • Delicious dessert-like taste
