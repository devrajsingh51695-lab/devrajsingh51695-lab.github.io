🍌 Banana Protein Smoothie

A creamy and delicious banana smoothie packed with protein. Perfect for breakfast, muscle recovery, and healthy weight gain.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • 4-5 ice cubes

Nutrition (Approx.)

  • Protein: 33g
  • Calories: 350
  • Carbohydrates: 34g
  • Fat: 6g

Instructions

  1. Add banana, milk, yogurt and protein powder to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again.
  4. Serve chilled.

Benefits

  • High in protein for muscle recovery
  • Provides natural energy from bananas
  • Rich in potassium
  • Great breakfast or post-workout drink
