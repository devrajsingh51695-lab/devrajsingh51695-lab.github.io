🍫 Chocolate Oat Protein Smoothie

Rich chocolate flavor, high protein content, and healthy oats make this smoothie perfect for breakfast, muscle gain, and post-workout recovery.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup oats
  • 4–5 ice cubes (optional)

Nutrition (Approx.)

  • Protein: 35g
  • Calories: 420
  • Carbohydrates: 40g
  • Fat: 8g

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice if desired.
  4. Serve chilled and enjoy.

Benefits

  • High in protein for muscle growth.
  • Provides slow-release energy from oats.
  • Great post-workout recovery drink.
  • Filling and nutritious breakfast option.
