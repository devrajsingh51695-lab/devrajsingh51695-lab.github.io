☕ Coffee Protein Smoothie

A creamy coffee smoothie packed with protein and natural energy. Perfect for breakfast, pre-workout fuel, and busy mornings.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso or ½ cup cold coffee
  • 1 scoop vanilla or chocolate protein powder
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4-5 ice cubes

Nutrition (Approx.)

  • Protein: 34g
  • Calories: 360
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 6g

Instructions

  1. Prepare and cool the coffee.
  2. Add coffee, banana, milk and protein powder to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Add ice cubes and blend again.
  5. Serve chilled.

Benefits

  • Provides natural caffeine energy
  • High in protein for muscle recovery
  • Perfect pre-workout drink
  • Quick and filling breakfast option
