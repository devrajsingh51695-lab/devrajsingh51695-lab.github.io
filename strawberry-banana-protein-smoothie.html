🍓🍌 Strawberry Banana Protein Smoothie

A sweet and creamy smoothie loaded with strawberries, bananas and protein. Perfect for breakfast, recovery and healthy muscle gain.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4-5 ice cubes

Nutrition (Approx.)

  • Protein: 34g
  • Calories: 370
  • Carbohydrates: 38g
  • Fat: 6g

Instructions

  1. Add strawberries, banana, milk and protein powder to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again.
  4. Serve chilled.

Benefits

  • High in protein for recovery
  • Rich in Vitamin C
  • Provides natural energy
  • Perfect breakfast smoothie
